Bistek (Filipino Beef Steak)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound Beef Sirloin - sliced thinly and tenderized
  • 1-2 shallots chopped
  • 1 large onion cut into rings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup calamansi or lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 2 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Place the beef slices in a bowl or container with lid. Add soy sauce, kalamansi or lemon juice, and chopped shallots. Cover and let it marinate.
  2. Heat oil over medium-high heat. Add the onion rings and cook according to preference. Just until slightly tender or totally caramelized. Remove from oil and set aside.
  3. On the same pan, add the beef and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side. Transfer cooked beef slices on a serving plate. Top with cooked onions.
  4. On the same pan over low heat, pour the soy sauce marinade. Add the cornstarch bring to a simmer while stirring constantly.
  5. Pour the sauce over the beef and onions. Serve.
