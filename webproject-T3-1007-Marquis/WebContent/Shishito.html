Blistered Shishito Peppers

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces shishito peppers
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 finely chopped shallot
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon togarashi
  • 1 lime wedge

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and pat the peppers dry on a clean tea towel. Transfer the peppers to a medium bowl, drizzle in the oil, and toss until the peppers are evenly coated. Set aside.
  2. Preheat a medium skillet over medium heat until itâ€™s hot enough that a drop of water sizzles and evaporates quickly on contact. Add the peppers and let them cook, stirring every minute or so
  3. In separate pan saute chopped shallots until soft then add butter, soy sauce, olive oil, lime juice.
  4. Add blistered peppers to sauce and toss. Finish dish on plate with togarashi pepper
